Frequently Asked Questions

1
Do I have to pay application fee?

This program is completely fee-free.

2
What are the early bird privileges ?

- The candidates submit early application will be prioritized to participate in Grow Beyond Seminar and Career Counseling.
Special Offers:
Candidates complete registration from 1/7 - 22/7 will receive a gift document package containing:
Stand out in an Assessment Center: A guide to the AC case studies
Effective resume crafting SEO-V
Cracking Behavioral interview question
How to Win Friends and Influence People
Business case studies collection
Candidates complete registration during the period from 23/7 - 1/8 will receive the following documents:
Effective resume crafting SEO-V
Cracking Behavioral interview question

3
Once I done submission, can I revise the registration or register again if I want to change my answers?

If you are not satisfied with your answers, please register again. However, you should carefully consider before applying to make sure your application has highest quality.

4
How do I know if I am selected to participate in Grow Beyond Seminar and Career Counseling?

We will send a confirmation email to the candidates selected to participate in Grow Beyond Seminar and Career Counseling.

5
If I do not receive a notification of participating in Grow Beyond Seminar and Career Counseling, am I eligible to join?

You still have a chance to participate though you do not receive our confirmation. From August 1st to August 8th, we will consider and extend the list of 30 - 50 peolple to participate in this special session.

6
If I were selected, would I be paired with a mentor in Career Counseling?

The matching will be based on the field, industry or occupation as your interest in the application. Information about your mentoring session will be included in your event confirmation.
Please follow SEO-Vietnam fanpage to update latest news about the event.

7
How long does the Career Counseling last?

This session will last maximum of 15 minutes.
In case of late arrival of candidates, it will be reduced respectively to ensure the rights of the other students.

8
Which language is spoken at this event?

The official language of the event is Vietnamese.
